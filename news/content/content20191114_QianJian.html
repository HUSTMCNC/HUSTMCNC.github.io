Qian Jian wins the Bronze Award for Grand Challenge in PCS2019

2019-11-14

Jian Qian achieved the Bronze Award for Grand Challenge in PCS2019, which is sponsored by Bytedance for the grand challenge to focus on identifying effective technologies beyond the state-of-the-art for short videos.

His team proposed a method called Dense Inception Attention Neural Network for In-Loop Filter which enhances both subjective and objective quality of short videos to take the challenge

The Picture Coding Symposium (PCS) is an international forum devoted specifically to advances in visual data coding. PCS is the pioneer conference, and has the longest history, in this field. Since 1969, PCS has provided the most exciting forum for the visual coding community from academia and industry.
