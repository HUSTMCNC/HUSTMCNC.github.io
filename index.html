Multimedia and Communication Network Center

  1. MCNC is an innovative center dedicating to the advancement of technology in the fields of multimedia infomation processing, wireless network, 3D video coding, social networks, cognitive radio,etc. Under the leadship of Prof.YU, more than twenty graduate and undergraduate members work together towards both academic and individual career fulfillment. Diversity of group members contributes to the active inter-communication within lab. Moreover, MCNC also provides a playground for members to have fun. Various entertaining activities and games are scheduled periodically, which help creating an ambient atmosphere among lab members.
