Dr. Kuan Zhang Gives a Talk

2018-06-28

On June 28th, Dr. Kuan Zhang from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln was invited to visit our school and made an academic report on Big Data and Security for Mobile Social Network at F401, WNLO.

Dr. Kuan Zhang first reviewed the development of social networks from the PC to the mobile. It is proposed that the current research on mobile social networks can be roughly divided into four directions and given many research topics in each direction. In the field of big data security, Dr. Kuan Zhang explained the prediction of infectious diseases in depth and put forward a new idea of combining health information with social information and encrypting the privacy of users.
